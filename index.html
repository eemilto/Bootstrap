About The Band

Michael Amott (Guitarist) formed Arch Enemy in 1995 with Johan Liiva (Arch Enemy's first singer). Since that AE has had 2 new vocalists (Angela Gossow and current Alissa White-Gluz). Tehy have released 11 studio albums, 4 live albums and also 5 EP's.
